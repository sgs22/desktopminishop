Built specifically to fit into any work environment, the Desktop Mini offers a timeless design for an elegant yet functional addition to any household or office space.

The Desktop Mini is sized so that it is suitable to sit on the desk while still holding modular parts for future proofing and repairability. The chassis has been designed to stand out and act as a feature within your workspace.

Pure Performance

The Desktop Mini utilises brand new CPU architecture and UHD Graphics to provide incredible speeds in both single and multi-core use cases. This allows it to outclass the new Apple M1 chip in multi-core performance whilst using less than 40 watts of power draw in a typical workload.

Passively Cooled

The Desktop Mini takes a niche approach to computer design. It doesn't feature any moving parts thanks to its passively cooled build, the heat generated by the processor is transferred to the large finned panel to be naturally dissipated, resulting in increased longevity and silent operation.
