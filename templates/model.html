Troubleshooting
      Boot Failure:
      Please check that you have no USB bootable disks connected already and use the window 10 boot repair to resolve you issue (this will automatically start after 3 failed boot attempts.
      Blank Computer Screen:
      Check that either the DisplayPort cable or HDMI cable is connected to your TV/Monitor correctly and that the TV/Monitor screen your using is powered on. If you see a display but Windows 10 is not booting then please see Windows 10 Troubleshooting page.
      Poor Internet Connections:
      Please check your internet is correctly working by looking at your router or seeing if you can access the internet on another device. Alternatively if your internet is slow but working try moving closer to you router or using a cabled solution.
      Issue not listed:
      Please issue a ticket through our contact email so that we can get back to you via email, text or phone to resolve your issue. Alternatively check the Windows 10 support page here.
